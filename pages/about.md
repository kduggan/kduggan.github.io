     <p>  <b>Kyle Duggan</b> is a Parliamentary reporter at <a href="http://www.ipolitics.ca">iPolitics.ca</a>, an online publication focused on Canadian politics and public policy. He specializes in federal health policy.</p>

  <p>Kyle's work has appeared in <i>The Hill Times</i> and on <i>CBC Ottawa Morning</i>.
  <br>He also interned at CBC national radio news, where he wrote scripts for <i>The World At Six</i> and <i>The World This Hour</i>.</p>

  <p>He went to Carleton University in Ottawa for the BA Honours program in Communications studies and the Master of Journalism program.</p>

  <p> Before going to university, he was the News Director at <i>CKNR 94.1 FM</i> in Elliot Lake, Ontario from 2008-2009, where he anchored newscasts and reported on local stories, including elections, city council decisions, regional demographic changes and, of course, Northern Ontario winters.</p>
  <p> Kyle has also reported for <i>AM 980</i> and Fanshawe College's student radio station <i>106.9 the "X" FM</i> in London, ON.
